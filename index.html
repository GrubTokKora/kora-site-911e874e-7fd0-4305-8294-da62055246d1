Authentic Hyderabadi Flavors

Welcome to Nawabi Hyderabad House, your home for authentic Hyderabadi cuisine in the Aurora/Naperville area. We are passionate about bringing you the rich, aromatic, and royal flavors of Hyderabad, prepared with love and the finest ingredients.

Our specialty is the world-renowned Hyderabadi dum biryani, but our menu extends to a wide variety of tandoori delights, flavorful curries, South Indian tiffins, and savory Indo-Chinese favorites. All our food is 100% halal, freshly prepared, and served in generous portions. Whether you're dining in, taking out, or planning an event, we promise an unforgettable culinary experience.
