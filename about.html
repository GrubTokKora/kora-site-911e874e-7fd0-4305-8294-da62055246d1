Authentic Hyderabadi

A Popular Indian Restaurant, Rooted in Hyderabad

Nawabi Hyderabad House is a beloved Indian restaurant in Aurora/Naperville, IL, specialising in the dish that made Hyderabad famous: fragrant dum biryani, layered with marinated meat or vegetables and served with cooling raita and spicy salan.

Beyond biryani, our kitchen turns out appetizers, curries, tandoori, dosas and desserts — from chicken tikka masala and butter chicken to crisp masala dosa and warm gulab jamun. Casual, warm and welcoming, with dine-in, takeout and delivery.

We believe great food shouldn't break the bank — affordable options, without compromising on taste.
